Useful words for studying documentation:

- **voltage**: tensão (electrical potential)
- **current**: corrente (flow of charge)
- **filter**: filtro (signal processing component)
- **throughput**: vazão (data transfer rate)
- **reliability**: confiabilidade (dependability)

A practical strategy is to read a short passage per day and note recurring expressions.
